About Raaga

RAAGA is a national level music festival organized by SWARA, the official music club of K L University. The music festival consists of several competitions, spot events and expos to celebrate music in different ways and to encourage people to participate in areas of their liking, symbolising that music is for everyone.

Raaga is a music festival with a variety of events, competitions and workshops that has a potential to make people of different interests and backgrounds to feel engaged and explore aspects of music
